Event Info

(All times are PST)

Join Game Design and Development at Berkeley for Bear Jams! Bear Jams is a 45-hr game jam open to everyone, not just UC Berkeley students!

This event is a hybrid setting game jam, where all submissions will have a live playthrough!

This means you can either join us in-person on the UC Berkeley campus or participate remotely through our Discord server!

And after the main jam is over, we will have a full Stream Week, where you can see your game played live, ending things off with our Showcase on Friday.

Participation is open to everyone, not just Berkeley students! There is no difference between teams with and without students! We're so excited to see all your wonderful games!

Our full Rules and Guidelines can be found here: tinyurl.com/BearJams-Rules
All participants MUST follow the Code of Conduct: tinyurl.com/BearJams-CodeOfConduct

If you are interested in participating, please check out the Itch.io and Discord!
