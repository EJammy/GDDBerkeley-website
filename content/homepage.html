Our Community

Join Cal's Game Design and Development Club to connect with fellow gamers. Whether you're a student, alumni, or have taken the decal, you're all welcome. Apply for a role, build a game team, and get mentorship from peers. We'll also host hackathons and guest speakers. Join us - we'd love to have you!

Decal

Our Video Game Design and Development Decal covers game creation from start to finish. Throughout the semester, students will pitch a game, form teams, and complete a project with instructor guidance. No prior game development experience is necessary, but basic art, music, or programming skills are required.
